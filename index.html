Accelarating South African Entrepreneurship

We are an entrepreneurial hub, a breeding ground for African technology where creativity is converted into successful businesses. The Cortex Hub is a technology incubator and research laboratory in the East London Industrial Development Zone for start-up and entrepreneurs. The Hub brings bright young minds from all over the Eastern Cape to establish their own businesses in the ICT domain. The Hub's mission is to expose young and ambitious entrepreneurs to the technology space by providing a platform for ideas and value creation. We plan to accelarate this world class entrepreneurship through a passion for innovation.
